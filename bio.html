I'm originally from Cincinnati, however I spent most of my 'formative years' in a small town called Williamsburg in Clermont County, Ohio. I live in a suburb of Cincinnati now with my husband and our (almost) three year old daughter, along with two dogs and a cat. I started college later in life, but that's ok! It gave me the opportunity to do my best. I attended Cincinnati State Community College and obtained an AS in Medical Laboratory Technology. I then completed a BS in Health Science from Muskingum University remotely. My professional background is in the Medical lab setting such as in hospitals and research organizations, and recently working for the Federal Government.
